Manual fallback

Use this if Chrome or Safari did not open automatically.

This link is designed to move out of Messenger and other in-app browsers before installation starts.

iPhone / iPad
  1. Tap the browser menu or share button in the in-app browser.
  2. Choose Open in Safari or Open in Browser.
  3. After Safari opens, continue the installer there.
Android
  1. Tap the menu in the in-app browser.
  2. Choose Open in Chrome or Open in Browser.
  3. After Chrome opens, continue the installer there.
