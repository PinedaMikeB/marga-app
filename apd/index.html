Encoder Guardrails

Use this quick reference before saving a payable or preparing a check.

Shared With Petty Cash APD Controlled
Use an expense account when the cost is already consumed for operations, such as fuel, rent, utilities, or repairs.
Use a liability account when the amount is still owed, such as installment arrangement, bank loan principal, or unpaid remittance.
Use an asset account when the cash was only transferred, stocked, or converted into a machine or fund, not yet expensed.
Never use Owner's Drawings as payroll Personal withdrawals must stay separate from salary, rent, utilities, and supplier payments.
